How to Play

Z3R Simulator

This tool was made for routing practice. Play seeds in 5 minutes instead of 2 hours!

Click on Green locations to open them. The Item Tracker and Log are updated automatically.

Click on Yellow locations to check what they are, but you're going to need something to get them.

Decide when to check the map or the medallions and freely warp between Light and Dark world by using the buttons below the Map. They are unlocked as you get the necessary items.

You can search the Item Log to remember where you got something, or filter out all those useless rupees.

You can play this just like you play a normal rando, thinking where you would be and what you would do, or just screw around and warp around the world getting everything.

Minor Glitches

If you activate Minor Glitches and Sequence Breaking, the following options are unlocked:

  • Dark Rooms
  • Fake Flippers
  • Water Walking
  • Super Bunny
  • Bomb Jumps
  • Hovering
